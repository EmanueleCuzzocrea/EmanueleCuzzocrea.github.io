Emanuele Cuzzocrea

Research

Analysing Locomotion Gaits for Quadruped Robots in Push-and-Slide Tasks

  • Explored the execution of a push-and-slide task using the ANYmal-D quadruped robot with a stick rigidly mounted to its base.
  • A hybrid control strategy is employed, combining both model-based and reinforcement learning
  • The objective is to determine the best gait, base orientation, and force values to perform the task.
  • Master's thesis at the PRISMA Lab.
  • Statistically significant differences between different configurations are assessed through analysis of variance.

Course Projects

Intelligent Control Methods for Robotic Arms

  • Train LNNs (Lagrangian Neural Networks) for obtaining the dynamic model of a 2-DoF robotic arm.
  • Apply PD and PD+ methods to the learned model.
  • Course project at ICS at TU Delft.
